                     HORIZON NATIONAL BANK — REFUND POLICY
                          Customer Support Division
                       Version 3.2 | Effective: Jan 1, 2025
================================================================================

1. OVERVIEW
-----------
Horizon National Bank is committed to resolving customer billing disputes and
transaction errors promptly and fairly. This policy governs how refunds,
provisional credits, and dispute adjustments are handled by customer support
agents across all service channels.

2. DUPLICATE TRANSACTION DISPUTES
----------------------------------
Customers reporting duplicate charges or transactions may be eligible for a
provisional credit within 3–5 business days following formal dispute initiation.
Agents must log the dispute in the CRM system and issue a Case Reference Number
to the customer before the call ends. Supporting documentation (e.g., bank
statements or screenshots) may be requested but is not mandatory to initiate a
provisional credit for amounts under $500.

3. UNAUTHORIZED TRANSACTION CLAIMS
------------------------------------
For transactions the customer did not authorize, agents must:
  a) Flag the account for fraud review immediately.
  b) Issue a provisional credit within 1–2 business days for amounts up to $2,500.
  c) Escalate amounts exceeding $2,500 to the Fraud Investigation Team.
  d) Advise the customer that final determination is made within 10 business days.

Provisional credits may be reversed if the investigation finds the transaction
to be legitimate.

4. MERCHANT DISPUTES
---------------------
If a customer did not receive goods or services paid for via debit/credit card:
  - A formal chargeback request must be initiated within 60 days of the
    transaction date.
  - Agents must verify that the customer first attempted resolution with the
    merchant directly.
  - Provisional credit is issued within 5–7 business days pending merchant
    response.
  - Merchants have 30 days to contest the chargeback before funds are finalized.

5. ATM & CASH DISPUTES
------------------------
Customers reporting ATM errors (e.g., cash not dispensed, incorrect amount) are
entitled to a provisional credit within 24 business hours. Agents must record the
ATM location, date, time, and claimed amount. ATM log review is automatically
triggered upon case creation.

6. REFUND ELIGIBILITY LIMITS
------------------------------
  - Agents may approve provisional credits up to $500 without supervisor sign-off.
  - Credits between $500–$2,500 require Team Lead approval within the same call
    session or via callback within 4 business hours.
  - Credits exceeding $2,500 must be routed to the Dispute Resolution Unit.

7. FEES & SERVICE CHARGE REFUNDS
----------------------------------
  - Agents may waive or refund up to two (2) monthly service fees or overdraft
    fees per customer account per calendar year as a goodwill gesture.
  - Waiving more than two fees requires supervisor authorization.
  - Annual fee refunds for premium accounts require Branch Manager approval.

8. REFUND TIMELINE COMMUNICATION
----------------------------------
Agents must clearly communicate the following to every customer with an open
dispute:
  a) Estimated credit timeline.
  b) Case Reference Number.
  c) How to check dispute status (online portal, app, or callback number).
  d) Right to submit additional documentation within 7 days of dispute initiation.

9. DOCUMENTATION & COMPLIANCE
-------------------------------
All refund and dispute actions must be logged in the CRM with:
  - Customer ID and verified account number.
  - Dispute category and claimed amount.
  - Provisional credit amount and date issued.
  - Agent ID and supervisor approval reference (if applicable).

Failure to document refund actions is subject to disciplinary review.

10. CONTACT FOR DISPUTES
-------------------------
Customers may also submit disputes via:
  - Online Banking Portal → "Dispute a Transaction"
  - Email: disputes@horizonnationalbank.com
  - Written correspondence: Horizon National Bank Dispute Center, PO Box 4820
